Biryani

Biryani is a rich and aromatic Indian rice dish cooked with spices, meat or vegetables, and layered with flavors. Perfect for special occasions!

Prep & Cook Time

  • Prep: 20 minutes
  • Cook: 40 minutes
  • Total: 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken or vegetables
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp yogurt
  • 2 tsp biryani masala
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 4 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee

Instructions

  1. Rinse and soak the rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the meat or vegetables with yogurt and spices until tender.
  3. Boil rice separately until 70% cooked.
  4. Layer rice over the cooked meat or vegetables in a pot.
  5. Cover tightly and cook on low heat (dum) for 15â€“20 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fried onions or fresh herbs, then serve hot.

Tips

You can add saffron soaked in milk for authentic aroma and color. Biryani tastes best when served with raita or salad.
