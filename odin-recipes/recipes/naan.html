Naan

Naan is a soft and fluffy Indian flatbread, perfect to accompany curries and gravies. Simple to make at home!

Prep & Cook Time

  • Prep: 15 minutes
  • Cook: 15 minutes
  • Resting: 1 hour
  • Total: 1 hour 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tbsp yogurt
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder in a bowl.
  2. Add yogurt and warm water gradually to form a soft dough.
  3. Knead the dough for 5â€“10 minutes, cover, and let it rest for 1 hour.
  4. Divide the dough into small balls and roll out into flat rounds.
  5. Cook on a hot skillet until bubbles form and both sides turn golden.
  6. Brush with ghee and serve warm.

Tips

For a fluffy texture, you can add a pinch of baking soda to the dough. Serve naan with curry or butter chicken.
