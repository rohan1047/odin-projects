Khichdi

Khichdi is a comforting, wholesome dish made from rice and lentils. Perfect for a light and healthy meal.

Prep & Cook Time

  • Prep: 10 minutes
  • Cook: 20 minutes
  • Total: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup yellow lentils (moong dal)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 4 cups water

Instructions

  1. Rinse rice and lentils thoroughly.
  2. Heat ghee in a pan, add cumin seeds until fragrant.
  3. Add rice, lentils, turmeric, and water. Mix well.
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15â€“20 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with optional ghee on top.

Tips

You can add vegetables like carrots and peas for extra nutrition.
